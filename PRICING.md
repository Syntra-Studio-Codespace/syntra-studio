# Global Pricing Notes

- Base currency: USD
- Currency display:
  - All prices are stored internally in USD.
  - Automatically detect the visitor's local currency using browser locale.
  - Display an estimated local currency equivalent beside the USD price.
  - Allow users to manually switch currencies from the navigation bar.
  - Final invoices are issued in the agreed billing currency.
- Supported display currencies:
  - USD (Default)
  - PKR
  - EUR
  - GBP
  - CAD
  - AUD
  - AED
  - SAR
  - INR
- Exchange rate source: Live exchange rates with periodic cache updates.
- Payment processing integration:
  - Stripe (where available)
  - Payoneer
  - Wise
  - Bank Transfer
- Taxes/fees:
  - Prices exclude applicable taxes and third-party transaction fees.
- Refund/cancellation policy:
  - 50% deposit required before work begins.
  - Deposits are non-refundable once development has commenced.
  - Remaining balance must be paid before final delivery.
- Currency:
  - USD (Canonical Pricing Currency)
- Bundle discounts:
  - 10% discount when purchasing a website together with a maintenance plan.
- Quote validity:
  - All quotations remain valid for 30 calendar days.
